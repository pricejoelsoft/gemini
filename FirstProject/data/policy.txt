Company Policy Manual (2025)

SECTION 1: WORK HOURS
All full-time staff must work 8 hours per day. Core hours are 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM. Flexible start times are available between 7:00 AM and 10:00 AM. Remote work is limited to three days per week.

SECTION 2: TRAVEL EXPENSE
All travel expenses over $500 require pre-approval from the department head. Mileage reimbursement is fixed at $0.67 per mile. Submit receipts to the 'Finance' drive folder.
